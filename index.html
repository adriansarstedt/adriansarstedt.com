About Me

Since a young age I have been enthralled with technology and its potential. I put together this website to share some of my personal projects and hopefully inspire fellow entrepreneurs.

I am currently studying Software Design at the University of Melbourne. Along with my studies I am exploring multiple business ideas and startup opportunities. This includes catering to clients with a broad range of software requests including web-design and mobile development. One day I hope to run my own company that has a positive impact on the world.

I BELIEVE IN NEW IDEAS

My personal projects range from a complex 3D rendering algorithms to the android kids game Word Chain. I believe each project is unique and important in its own right.

Dont hesitate to contact me if you have any ideas you would like to discuss, no matter the medium.

I pride myself on an ability to consider a project from all angles and ultimately determine the approach. My extensive experience in design, programming and marketing have proven useful in many fields.
